❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a playlist?

To create a playlist, select multiple songs from your library and use the "Add to Playlist" option. You can name your playlist and organize your music exactly how you want it.

Can I use this app offline?

Yes! Player-Offline-Unlimited is designed for offline use. You can listen to your entire music library without an internet connection. However, streaming features from Jamendo require an active internet connection for the first download.

What audio formats are supported?

Player-Offline-Unlimited supports MP3, FLAC, WAV, AAC, and many other popular audio formats. Check the app settings for the complete list of supported formats.

How do I use the equalizer?

The equalizer can be accessed from the main player interface. Choose from preset modes (Bass Boost, Rock, Pop, etc.) or manually adjust individual frequency bands for custom sound tuning.

Is my music data backed up?

All your playlists and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal music files on our servers. You are responsible for backing up your device if you want to preserve this data.

How do I report a bug?

If you encounter any bugs or technical issues, please email us at hikhang2025@gmail.com with a detailed description of the problem, your device model, and app version.

Can I request a new feature?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. Please send your feature requests and suggestions to hikhang2025@gmail.com. We review all feedback and consider it for future updates.

Why does the app require permissions?

The app requires permission to access your device's storage to read and play audio files, and to manage your playlists. These permissions are necessary for the app to function properly and do not compromise your privacy.

Is Player-Offline-Unlimited free?

Yes, Player-Offline-Unlimited is completely free to use with all core features included. We may offer premium features or ad-free versions in the future, but the app is free to download and use today.

How can I improve app performance?

To optimize performance: clear your cache regularly, ensure you have enough storage space, close background apps, and keep your device's OS updated. For specific issues, contact our support team.
