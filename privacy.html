Last updated: March 1, 2026

1. Information Collection

Player-Offline-Unlimited ("we", "our", or "us") does not collect any personal identifiable information (PII). We do not require you to create an account to use our basic services.

Local Data: Any playlists or favorites you create are stored locally on your device. We do not access or collect this data.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymous diagnostic data to improve app performance and fix crashes. This data is never linked to your identity.

2. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates third-party APIs such as Jamendo. These services may collect information as per their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them:

  • Jamendo Personal Use License

3. Offline Mode

Our offline playback feature accesses your device's local storage to play audio files. We do not upload your personal files to any servers. All content playback happens entirely on your device.

4. Data Security

We take data security seriously. Since we do not collect personal information, there is minimal risk exposure. Your local files remain under your complete control and are never transmitted to our servers.
